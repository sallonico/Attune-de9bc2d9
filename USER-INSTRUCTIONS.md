# User Instructions

Welcome to Attune — a medication-adherence app that takes the guesswork out of *when* to take your medications and makes your consistency visible over time. This guide walks you through getting set up. Onboarding is a short, 5-step flow; the steps below follow it in order.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

- Open the Attune app and sign up with your email and password.
- Follow the on-screen prompts to complete setup. Your timezone is detected automatically from your browser, so your dose times stay correct wherever you are.

### 2. Enter Your Name

- Tell Attune your name so the app can personalize your dashboard.

### 3. Add Your Medications

Enter the medications you want to track. You can add **1 to 5 medications**, and you can add or remove them later from Schedule settings (you'll always keep at least one).

If you're testing the device, you can use example medications such as:

- Lisinopril
- Metformin
- Atorvastatin
- Levothyroxine
- Amlodipine

### 4. Set Each Medication's Schedule

Each medication gets **its own independent schedule** — pick the time and the days of the week for each one. Two medications can share the same time or sit at completely different times.

- Think about when you normally take each medication.
- Add the appropriate days and times in the app.
- Review your schedule to make sure it matches your routine.

### 5. Set Your Daily Routine

Tell Attune about your typical day — when you wake up, when you go to sleep, whether a medication is taken with food, and your meal times. This **routine is shared across all your medications** and is used to fit each dose to your real day. Attune will also flag conflicts (for example, a dose scheduled outside your awake hours, or a with-food dose that isn't near a meal).

### 6. Choose Your Features

During onboarding you can turn optional features on or off. You can revisit these later:

- **Wellness check-ins** — after logging a dose, record how you feel physically and emotionally (1–5) with an optional note.
- **Insights** — a free, rule-based pattern detector that surfaces things like a recurring weekday you tend to skip.
- **Caregiver view** — share a summary (adherence %, missed doses, mood/physical averages, and an alert after consecutive misses) with a family member or doctor. This is off by default.

## Using Attune Day to Day

### Record a Dose

When it's time for a medication, press the **Taken** button on that medication's card to log it. Each medication is logged independently, once per day.

- If you enabled **wellness check-ins**, a short modal appears after you mark a dose taken so you can note how you're feeling.

### Snooze a Reminder

Not ready yet? Use **Remind me later** to snooze. After 3 snoozes for the same medication, Attune automatically logs that dose as missed so your records stay honest.

### Track Your Progress

- Your dashboard shows a **30-day adherence trend** across all medications and a **history heatmap** of recent days.
- Each medication card shows its **next due dose** and a **7-day upcoming forecast**.

## Test the Device Connection

The optional Attune pill device connects to your browser directly over Bluetooth Low Energy.

- Connect the device to your computer using the USB-C cable to power it on.
- Button does not work (working on it)
- Open the app in a supported browser — Web Bluetooth requires **Chrome, Edge, or Opera** and a secure context (`https` or `http://localhost`).
- Select **Attune Connect** and follow the prompts to verify that the Bluetooth connection is working properly.

## Notes

- These steps help you get familiar with the device and app. The complete workflow includes the additional features and setup options covered above and in the [README](README.md).
- If you encounter connection issues, ensure the device is powered on and the USB-C cable is securely connected, confirm you're using a supported browser, then retry **Attune Connect**.
